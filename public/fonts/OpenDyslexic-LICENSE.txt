OpenDyslexic
============

Typeface designed to increase readability for readers with dyslexia.
Created by Abelardo Gonzalez. https://opendyslexic.org

Files in this directory:
  OpenDyslexic-Regular.woff  (Version 2.001)
  OpenDyslexic-Bold.woff     (Version 2.001)
