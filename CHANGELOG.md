# Changelog

All notable changes, newest first. Source of truth; user-facing notes are distilled from this.

## 2026.06.1 — 2026-06-16

- Recovery screen: if the app ever fails to load, it now shows a calm "Načíst znovu" screen instead of a blank page, and one tap refreshes to a clean version. Your progress stays on the device.

## 2026.06 — 2026-06-16

First public release.

- Czech QWERTZ touch-typing course — 7 stages, 32 lessons on a soft-suggested path; nothing is locked, everything stays replayable.
- Targeted weak-key drills from your most-missed keys, free/custom-text practice, and a relaxed playground.
- Informational accuracy and CPM (úhozy/min), framed against your own past — no leaderboards, ranks, or streaks.
- Local-first: all data stays on the device (Evolu), no account or email; optional cross-device sync via a recovery phrase, off by default.
- Offline-capable PWA with self-hosted fonts (zero third-party requests) and an optional dyslexia-friendly font.
- Accessibility: shape-and-label finger coding (not hue alone), light/dark/system themes, adjustable text size, reduced-motion support.
- Agent discovery: robots.txt welcoming AI crawlers, sitemap.xml, and an llms.txt index.
